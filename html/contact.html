Helpful sites that make contacting reps easier!

Show Me State Action!
Learn which bills in state legislature require immediate action. Includes filters to find bills by issue and video tutorials to get started with state-level activism.
5 Calls
Excellent phone app--choose issues you care about and get scripts for making calls to congress.
Countable
Get clear, concise summaries of bills going through Congress, see what others think, then take action. Telling your reps how you feel is easier than ever with email and now video messages. Make your democracy more responsive!
Congress.org
Sign up for MegaVote to get email updates about how your senators and representative voted in the past week, as well as what they will be voting on this week.
